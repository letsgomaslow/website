AI Impact on Business Areas

Gartner insights show that the benefits of AI in enterprises can be broken down into three core areas:

  • Employee Productivity: Up to 50% improvement.
  • Process Improvement: Approximately 30% efficiency gains.
  • Business Model Innovation: About 20% driven by strategic transformation.

These figures represent the potential gains from a well-executed AI strategy, turning technology investments into measurable business outcomes.
