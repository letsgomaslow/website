Data Everywhere and Every Kind

Data is the lifeblood of AI. In modern enterprises, data comes in many forms—structured and unstructured, digital and analog—from sources such as CRM systems, IoT devices, ERP platforms, emails, and more.

Key Points:

  • Diversity: Integrates both structured and unstructured data.
  • Integration: Consolidates data from multiple sources to ensure a unified view.
  • Quality: Focuses on data cleaning and standardization to fuel effective AI models.

Embedded AI

Embedded AI refers to the current organizational tools that are beginning to integrate AI into their software. Examples include platforms like Salesforce and ServiceNow, where AI capabilities are gradually being embedded to enhance existing functionalities.

This section is provided solely to help you understand the concept of Embedded AI. We do not offer any services in this area.

BYOAI (Bring Your Own AI)

Employees across all departments are increasingly using their own AI tools. While this trend promotes innovation, it also introduces challenges in consistency, security, and integration.

Key Points:

  • Decentralized Adoption: Use of external AI tools by employees.
  • Varied Impact: Different departments may experience varied efficiencies and risks.
  • Mitigation Opportunity: Standardize AI usage with robust open-source solutions to manage risks.

TRiSM – Trust, Risk, and Security Management

TRiSM ensures that your AI initiatives are secure, transparent, and compliant. It safeguards your operations by addressing potential risks—from data breaches to algorithmic bias.

Key Points:

  • Explainability: Transparent and understandable AI decisions.
  • ModelOps: Effective operationalization and management of AI models.
  • Data Anomaly Detection: Identifying inconsistencies in data.
  • Adversarial Attack Resistance: Protection against malicious attacks.
  • Data Protection: Ensuring privacy and compliance.

Built AI

Built AI refers to custom-developed AI solutions tailored for your enterprise's unique challenges. These solutions integrate seamlessly with your existing systems to deliver maximum value.

Key Points:

  • Customization: Solutions designed for your specific needs.
  • Integration: Seamless incorporation into your current workflows.
  • Innovation: Drives unique business outcomes through tailored AI strategies.

Centralized Data

Centralized data forms the backbone of your AI initiatives, ensuring that all layers of your AI ecosystem are supported by clean, accessible, and unified data.

Key Points:

  • Unified Repository: Central data lakes or warehouses consolidate all data.
  • Consistency: Ensures uniform data quality and accessibility across the organization.
  • Support: Provides a solid foundation for AI model training and deployment.
